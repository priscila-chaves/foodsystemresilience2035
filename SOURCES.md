## Verification Methodology

All claims were categorised as:

- **✓ VERIFIED** — Confirmed by primary source with direct citation
- **~ PARTIALLY VERIFIED** — Core claim confirmed but specific figures unverifiable
- **? UNVERIFIABLE** — Unable to locate supporting primary source
- **✗ INCORRECT** — Contradicted by primary sources (with correction provided)

## Data Currency

- Knowledge cutoff: January 2026
- Investment figures: Calendar year 2024 (most recent complete data)
- Regulatory status: As of January 2026

## Limitations

- European adoption percentages for specific technologies (e.g., "18% UK farm robotics adoption") could not be independently verified
- Some market projections rely on analyst estimates with varying methodologies
- Consumer attitude data draws from multiple surveys with different sampling approaches
